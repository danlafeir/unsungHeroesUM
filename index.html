About Unsung Heroes

This organization is dedicated to recognizing the unsung heroes on campus. This can be a supervisor that goes above and beyond because they care. A professor that makes sure every student's question is answer regardless if OH are over.This organization wants to recognize these people for the difference they make in people's lives.

To do this we want to help you or a group of people show your appreciation. Contact us and tell us about someone in your life that is making a difference and we will work together to recognize that person for who they are.
